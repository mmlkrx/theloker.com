• Expert Finder

    Where to turn if you want to learn something new about an industry or topic? How to weed through the obscene amount of information that exists online. Ideally one could turn to an expert that gives pointers to reliable information and materials. This could be a process first, maybe a website later.

  • Instamania

    A bot to organically grow an engaged following on instagram. Recently instagram has been restricting their public APIs, the goal of the bot would be to leverage the private API and completely escape detection by instagram.

  • Proof of Existence

    A simple website that allows people to submit any document or picture and store the data's SHA on the ethereum blockchain. Should integrate with Metamask to make submitting the transaction easy.

  • Ghost Facebook

    Detailed instructions how to download all of your data off of facebook and then delete everything and only keep a ghost profile on facebook to still be able to use messenger and events.

  • Fun Units

    A small tool for writers that need to convert arbitrary numbers into more visual units. I.e. 5.000 football fields, 7 times Manhatten island, 33.000 bottles of coke, 30 elephants, etc.

  • Writescreen

    Screenplay writing software based on the fountain markdown format, backed by a git repository. Version control screenplays and integrate a process for writing exercises and organizing your notes and ideas around the story.

  • Backstage

    Media management software for film productions. Media managers can determine what behind the scenes photos and footage is permitted to be released. Then crew and actors can access released photos and share with their social media following

  • Broadcast

    Broadcast helps anyone publish a high quality podcast. It provides best practices, makes sure podcasts are published on itunes, deploys a website for your podcast, helps you with transcribing podcast episodes and recommends the right equipment as well as best practices and websites to use for remote interviewing guests etc.

  • Dolores

    A teachable agent you can train whenever you're learning something new. Dolores is a simple AI you can teach by building her a mind map around core concepts. She will be able to take tests and answer questions about the topic you're teaching her.

  • Grocery Alert

    Platform that lets people purchase supermarket groceries that are about to meet their date of expiry. Home delivery at night with bike messengers.
